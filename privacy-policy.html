Privacy Policy for RemindSafe Last updated: 2026 RemindSafe respects your privacy and is designed to keep your data secure. Information Collection RemindSafe does not collect personal information from users. The application works primarily offline and does not transmit personal data to external servers. Local Data Storage All reminders, notes, and documents stored in the vault remain on the user's device. The application does not upload or store this data remotely. Data Sharing RemindSafe does not automatically share any user data. Users may manually share reminders using external messaging applications if they choose. Backup and Restore Users may export and import backups of their data. Backup files are stored only where the user chooses on their device. Security The application does not access or collect sensitive information beyond what is required for its functionality. Children’s Privacy RemindSafe does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Changes to This Privacy Policy This privacy policy may be updated in future versions of the application.
